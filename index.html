Our Story

More than a club — a brotherhood forged on friendship, sportsmanship, and the beautiful game.

The Foundation

Born from the dusty pitches of Siddheswari Balur Math in Dhaka, Helix United was officially founded on August 3, 2019. What started as a collective of ambitious local talents quickly evolved into a structured, competitive footballing institution.

Our founders envisioned a platform where raw talent could be honed into tactical brilliance, without ever losing the sheer joy of the beautiful game. Today, we stand as one of Dhaka's most passionate grassroots clubs.
